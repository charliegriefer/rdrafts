Choosing the right subreddit

There are thousands of subreddits available to hold your AMA, each dedicated to paticular topic. Use the subreddit search to find a subreddit that best matches the topic of your AMA. If you want reach a general audience, choose r/IAmA (great for famous people, big businesses, or anyone that has a truly unique experience to share).

More Tips:

  • Take time to learn about the subreddit before committing to it. See what redditors are talking about and how they talk to each other.
  • Look in the sidebar to see how many subscribers are in the subreddit. Depending on your goals, a smaller subreddit might not be worth your time.
  • Read the rules of the subreddit to make sure that it doesn't specifically ban AMA's or have any other rules that you should pay attention to.

Writing a great title

The title of your AMA should grab redditors attention and briefly describe who you are. You can mention a notable accomplishment, an upcoming event that you are involved in, a free product/service giveaway, or highlight your field of expertise.

More Tips:

  • You cannot edit the title after you submit your AMA. Proofread it multiple times before submitting.
  • Include "AMA" at the end of your title. This let's redditors know that you are doing an AMA.
  • Do not express cockiness or arrogance in your AMA title (unless that is part of your brand).
  • Do not promote anything in your title unless you are giving away something for free or announcing something that the audience cares deeply about.

Writing a great body

The body text will be shown to redditors after they click on your AMA. Use the body of your AMA to elaborate on who you are, what you have accomplished, and why you are doing the AMA. You can use the body of your AMA to guide questions towards a specific topic to keep the AMA focused (but never make a topic off-limits).

More Tips:

  • Feel free to promote something near the end of the body. Don't mention a specific product or service unless it is accompanied by a reddit-specific discount code (e.g. enter the code "reddit" for 33% off).
  • You can edit the body of your AMA at any time. That being said, only make minor corrections and if you want to add something substantial, place it at the end and label it with the word "Edit" in bold.
  • Tell the audience that you are excited for the AMA and thank them for the opportunity.
  • Format the body of your AMA using reddit markdown. Breaking your copy into paragraphs is a must, but also consider using bold text and hyperlinks.

Creating a verification link

Verifying your AMA adds credibility to your submission and will increase engagement with redditors.

To verify your AMA, make a post on your official website or on your favorite social media account that includes your reddit username and a short "hello" to the AMA participants. For bonus points, include a picture of yourslef holding up a "Hi reddit".
