Choosing the right subreddit

There are thousands of subreddits to choose from, each dedicated to paticular topic. Use the subreddit search to find a subreddit that best matches the topic of your AMA. If you want reach a general audience, choose r/IAmA.

IMPORTANT:

  • Learn about a subreddit before doing an AMA there. Read the sidebar and latest posts to get a feel for the community.
  • Search the phrase "title:ama" using the search at the top right of the subreddit. This will search for AMAs that have been done previously in that subreddit.
  • Read the rules of the subreddit to see if there are any rules or guidelines for AMAs.
  • If you have any doubts, message the moderators to let them know about your plans to do an AMA. Look for the "message the moderators" link in the sidebar of the subreddit.

Writing a great title

The title of your AMA should grab redditors' attention and briefly describe who you are.

IMPORTANT:

  • You cannot edit the title after you submit your AMA. Proofread it multiple times before submitting.
  • Briefly explain who you are in the title (e.g. "I am John Doe. AMA." is a bad title).
  • Include "AMA" at the end of your title.
  • Do not promote anything in your title unless you are doing something that is extremely valuable to the community.

Writing a great body

The body will be shown to redditors after they click on your AMA. Use the body of your AMA to elaborate on who you are, what you have accomplished, and why you are doing the AMA. You can use the body of your AMA to guide questions towards a specific topic, but remember that no topic is off-limits.

IMPORTANT:

  • You can edit the body of your AMA any time after it is submitted, but only make minor corrections. If you want to add something substantial, place it at the end and label it with the word "Edit" in bold.
  • Express gratitude. Thank the community for allowing you to spend time with them.
  • Format the body of your AMA using reddit markdown. Breaking your copy into paragraphs is a must, but also consider using bold text and hyperlinks.
  • Feel free to promote something near the end of the body. Reddit is generally more accepting of general links to your social media accounts or website. Do not link a specific product or service unless it is accompanied by a reddit-specific discount code (e.g. enter the code "reddit" for 33% off).

Creating a verification link

Verifying your AMA is required. It proves to the community that you are who you say you are.

Options for verifying your AMA:

  • Make a post on your official website or favorite social media account announcing the AMA. The post should include your reddit username and the subreddit you have chosen for your AMA (e.g. "I'm doing a reddit AMA on Tuesday in r/IAmA. My reddit username is /u/reddit_username.").
  • Take a picture of yourself holding up a sign that says "Hi reddit" and includes your reddit username. Upload it to imgur to create a verification link you can share with reddit.
  • Some moderators will verify you before your AMA and tag your AMA as "verified". In this case, it is still good to create a verification link using one of the methods above.
